Pins CS-0019 (E.5): an indented line whose first word is a top-level keyword (here, `recipe inner`) is a `Content` token by the column-0 rule (§{lexical.keywords}) and dispatches as a `shell_command` inside the enclosing recipe body — it does NOT terminate the outer recipe. The outer recipe body has three shell steps: `echo before`, `recipe inner` (literal command for the user's shell), `echo after`. The recipe terminates at EOF.
